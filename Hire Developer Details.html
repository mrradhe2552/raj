Job Description

Responsibilities:


• To plan, execute, and manage marketing campaigns from ideation to completion.
• Managing in house SEO activities-On page and Off page both.
• Managing all aspects of Technical SEO.
• Develop optimized strategies that increase the company's search engine results rankings.
• Plan high-quality content that conveys technical and functional concepts in a voice that targets our audience.
• Responsible for managing activities such as link building and keyword strategy to increase rankings on all major search networks.
• Performing competitive research, and prepare strategies that how we can get benefit from the analysis.
• Track and analyze the performance of digital marketing efforts using a variety of Web analytics tools (Google Analytics, WebTrends etc.).
• Launch optimized online adverts through Google Adwords, Facebook etc. to increase company and brand awareness.
• Collaborate with others to manage SEO & Digital Marketing strategy.
• Monitor daily performance metrics to understand SEO performance and provide suggestions for improvement.
• Stay up to date with the latest SEO and digital marketing trends and best practices.
• To brainstorm new and creative business growth strategies and perform market research to learn more about new products and services, concepts, and find new content.

Required skills and experience:


• Excellent understanding of digital marketing concepts and best practices
• Experience with social media, Google Adwords and email campaigns and SEO/SEM
• Strong knowledge of On page and Off page SEO
• Able to do competitive analysis
• Hands on Experience with various tools like Google Analytics, Semrush, Keyword Planner etc
• Good Analytical Skill
• Critical Thinking Skill
• Excellent written and verbal english communication skills
